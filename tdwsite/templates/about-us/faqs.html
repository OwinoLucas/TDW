What Types Of Documents Do You Store?

We can store any document, including legal documents, financial records, marketing materials, and more.

How Do You Ensure The Safety Of My Documents?

We take the safety of your documents very seriously. Our facility has advanced fire suppression systems and 24/7 surveillance to prevent accidents or breaches.

Can I Access My Documents Online?

Yes, we offer online access to your documents through a secure portal. This allows you to view and retrieve documents from any device with an internet connection.

How Do You Organize And Index My Documents?

We offer customised organisation and indexing solutions to fit your specific needs. This includes using barcodes, labels, and digital systems to help you quickly locate and access your documents.

Do You Offer Document Shredding Services?

Yes, we offer secure document shredding services to help you dispose of sensitive documents safely and responsibly.

How often do you conduct inventory management and document purging?

We conduct regular inventory management and document purging to ensure that your storage is organised and efficient. We can work with you to determine the frequency of these services based on your specific needs.

How Much Does Document Storage Cost?

The cost of our document storage services varies based on the volume of documents you need to store and the duration of storage. We offer competitive pricing and are happy to provide a quote based on your specific needs.

How Do I Retrieve My Documents?

You can retrieve your documents in person by visiting our facility during business hours or requesting to have them delivered.

Do You Offer Pickup And Delivery Services?

Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services for your convenience.

Is Your Facility Climate-Controlled?

Our facility has climate-controlled storage units to protect your documents from extreme temperature fluctuations. This helps to ensure the longevity and integrity of your documents.
