Fairseat Foundation

Fairseat Foundation, just outside Nairobi, embraces residents in a tranquil haven within the secure embrace of Rosslyn Lonetree Estate. Residents enjoy privacy and independence, with limited nursing care available, ensuring immediate assistance if required. It's distinct from a hospital or nursing home, and applicants undergo a medical examination for community acceptance.
