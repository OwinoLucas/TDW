M-Files Electronic Document

Management Solutions (EDMS)

The Document Warehouse is your trusted onsite electronic document management system (EDMS) solutions provider. With our expertise and M-Files’ power, we offer you a seamless and efficient way to manage your documents.

What is M-Files? M-Files is a leading EDMS software that revolutionises how organisations handle their documents. It provides a centralised platform to store, organise, and retrieve your electronic files effortlessly.

Why choose M-Files for your document management needs? Let us highlight some key benefits:


  1. Improves workflow
  2. Increases information reuse
  3. Eliminates redundancy
  4. One single document management solution that integrates with Windows
  5. Document control

Intelligent Metadata: M-Files utilises a unique metadata-driven approach, eliminating the need for complex folder structures. This means you can easily classify and find documents based on their properties, such as author, client, or project, saving you valuable time and improving accuracy.

Version Control: Say goodbye to confusion caused by multiple versions of the same document. M-Files keeps track of document versions, allowing you to easily access the latest version and revert to previous versions if needed.

Workflow Automation: Streamline your business processes with M-Files’ automated workflows. Define custom workflows to ensure documents follow the right approval processes, notifications are sent at the appropriate stages, and tasks are completed efficiently.

Secure Access and Permissions: M-Files ensures that your documents are secure and accessible only to authorised personnel. Set granular permissions to control who can view, edit, or delete specific documents, providing an additional layer of confidentiality and compliance.

Integration Capabilities: M-Files seamlessly integrates with other essential tools and software you may already be using, such as Microsoft Office, SharePoint, and CRM systems. This integration enhances collaboration, boosts productivity, and simplifies document management across your organisation.
