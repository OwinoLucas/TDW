Backup Storage: Protecting Your Data in the Digital Age

Backup storage is a crucial aspect of data management for any organisation, large or small. It ensures that essential data is safe and secure in unforeseen circumstances, such as a natural disaster or a cyber attack. This article will explore the various backup storage options available, their advantages and disadvantages, and how to choose the best one for your organisation.


What is Backup Storage?

Backup storage refers to creating copies of your digital data and storing them separately. The purpose of backup storage is to provide a way to recover your data if it is lost, corrupted, or deleted. Our solution allows you to store your backup storage media (such as backup tapes) in our secure temperature and humidity-controlled vault: backup storage media and External hard drives.


Backup Tapes

Backup tapes are a traditional method of storing data on magnetic tape. It is a cost-effective solution for large organisations that need to store large amounts of data.


Choosing the Right Backup Storage for Your Organization

The proper backup storage for your organisation depends on several factors, such as the amount of data you need to store, your budget, and your organisation’s data recovery needs.

Most importantly, the environment where this backup storage media is stored ensures that your backup media is protected and easily accessible to ensure business continuity in your organisation.

Physical storage of your backup media in our vault is a cost-effective way of ensuring that your organisation has peace of mind regarding business continuity in case of any lost data.
